An Amateur Suverying Tool

This app is inspired by the hills of San Francisco. It started out because of San Francisco's parking laws require you to curb your tires if a street has more than a 3% grade. It turns out that knowing how steep and tall a hill is can make it more fun to climb to the top. Take a walk and do some amateur survey work along the way.


What Does this Do?

At the most basic level this app uses your phone's sensors to measure how steep a hill is.
You can either use your phone as a level or you can use the camera to target a specific point (like the top of a hill).
Additionally, it displays your lat-long location and elevation. You can save several points along your walk to map out a larger region. You can also export this as a csv if you want to do a more sophisticated analysis.
