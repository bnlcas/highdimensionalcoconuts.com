Pyongyang Tomorrow Here and Now!

Modify your photos' exif metadata to be taken tomorrow in Pyongyang, North Korea 100 meters below sea level!


What Does this Do?

Pyongyang Tomorrow makes a copy of a photo whose metadata is edited to read that the photo was taken in Pyongyang, North Korea, 100 meters below sea level one day in the future. This helps protect your privacy and thwart attempts to track your location based on your photos' metadata. You can find the source code on Github.
You could also just use exiftool on a desktop computer's terminal:


Command Line Version:

exiftool -GPSLatitude=39.0738-GPSLatitudeRef=N -GPSLongitude=125.8198 -GPSLongitudeRef=E -GPSAltitude=-100 -GPSAltitudeRef="Below Sea Level" -AllDates="$(date -v +1d '+%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S')" MY_PHOTO.jpg
